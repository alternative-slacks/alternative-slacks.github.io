About

The fake news bot is a Slack bot that provides information about questionable news sources. It searches over 400 fake news sources based on user input. Once the bot receives a successful query, it returns information about the fake news source's title, URL, category (satire, clickbait, conspiracy, etc.) and political alignment.

The fake news bot uses data from the Fake News Detector GitHub repository.
