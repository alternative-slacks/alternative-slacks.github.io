  • get *publication title* searches for fake news source based on title of publication
  • get *URL* searches for source based on URL

About

The fake news bot is a Slack bot that provides information about questionable news sources. Once the bot receives a successful query, it returns information about the fake news source's title, URL, and political alignment.

Created by alternative-slacks, a Walrus Chingu cohort.
